Pricing

Customize your listening with our pricing range. You can have many features as price varies. Our music app offers flexible pricing plans tailored to suit every listener's needs. Whether you're a casual music lover or an avid audiophile, you can choose from a range of options - starting with a free plan that gives you access to basic features, all the way to pro and artist plans that unlock high-quality streaming, offline downloads, and ad-free listening. With transparent pricing and no hidden fees, our goal is to bring you the best music experience at a price that fits your lifestyle.
